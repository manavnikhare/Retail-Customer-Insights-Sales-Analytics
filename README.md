# Retail Customer Insights & Sales Analytics

## 📌 Project Overview

Retail businesses generate large amounts of customer and transaction data, but raw data alone cannot provide meaningful business insights.
This project analyzes retail customer purchasing behavior, sales performance, customer demographics, subscription trends, and shopping preferences.
The objective is to transform raw customer transaction data into actionable insights using **Python, SQL, and Power BI**.

The project follows an end-to-end analytics workflow:
**Python → SQL → Power BI**

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

A retail company wants to better understand its customers and purchasing patterns.

The analysis focuses on:

- Identifying customer purchasing behavior
- Understanding sales performance across categories and locations
- Evaluating subscription customer behavior
- Analyzing customer demographics
- Understanding shopping preferences
- Comparing purchase behavior across different customer segments

The goal is to support **data-driven decisions related to customer engagement, sales performance, and retail strategy**.
